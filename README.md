## Danish Retail Promotion Stockouts — BI Case Study

### Business Context
Danish grocery retailers run frequent promotions. When demand is under-forecast,
stores stock out, customers are lost, and revenue and sustainability targets are impacted.

### Business Question
Which promotions cause stockouts, where do they occur, and how much revenue is lost
due to forecasting gaps?

### Dataset
Synthetic but Denmark-realistic promotion data:
- 25 stores across Sjælland and Jylland
- 6 product categories
- 16 promotion weeks
- One row per Store × Product × Promotion Week

### Key KPIs
- Lost Revenue (DKK)
- Promotion Stockout Rate (%)
- Forecast Error (MAPE)
- Promotion Weeks Analyzed

### Output
A Power BI dashboard highlighting:
- Total revenue lost due to stockouts
- Product categories driving the largest losses
- The relationship between forecast accuracy and financial impact

### Outcome
The dashboard helps commercial and supply-chain teams prioritize forecasting
improvements where they have the greatest economic return.
